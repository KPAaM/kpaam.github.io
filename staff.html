Peter Jan Sincak (PhD student)

Background

Peter Jan Sincak received MSc and Bc degrees from Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, Technical University of Kosice, in Industrial Mechatronics. His master and bachelor thesis was focusing on novel in-pipe robot mechanisms with the applications in chimney cleaning. His current research is focusing on application of machine learning based control algorithms in the area of continuum robotics.

Background

Tomas Merva received his BSc and MSc degrees from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Technical University of Kosice, both in electrical drives (control and construction of electric motors, power electronics, microprocessors).
